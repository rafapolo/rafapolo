About

Here you can find a collection of material from selected Producers around protecting yourself online. Explore by using the filters above to watch films and animations, play games, and read online resources. Read more about the project here.

This project was designed by Tactical Technology Collective and supported by Level Up to bring together a collection of material around protecting yourself online. These projects have been curated and have been produced by a number of different individuals and organisations including Tactical Technology Collective, Front Line Defenders, EFF, Open Data City, The Tor Project, The Centre for Investigative Journalists and Access Now. We thought it would be good to have them on a single page - as we use them alot at workshops, trainings and presentations. If you think we should add something here please let us know. We are working on adding other than English languages as well.
